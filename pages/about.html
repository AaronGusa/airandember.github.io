About Air&Ember

Hi there, and welcome!

I’m Aaron, the guy behind the keyboard, designing and developing websites for small businesses like yours.

The name “Air and Ember” has a special meaning—it’s a playful nod to my wife Amber and myself, Aaron (see what we did there?). While it’s a one-man show on the web design front, Amber is my biggest cheerleader and occasional brainstorm partner.

The air and ember theme reflects how we approach life and work: light and adaptable like air, but with the fire and spark of a glowing ember. Nature inspires us, and we aim to build websites that feel effortless yet full of life. Let’s create something amazing together!

Why Air & Ember

I bring a unique blend of creativity and technical skill to the table, backed by a BS in Applied Technology and certifications in web development, computer programming, and system administration. With a strong foundation in technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and experience with advanced tools such as MEAN and MERN stacks, Firebase, and Python, I specialize in building dynamic and efficient websites for small businesses.

What sets me apart is my ability to seamlessly combine design and development. As both an artistic creative and a coding problem-solver, I eliminate the need for multiple teams, streamlining the process and keeping costs manageable for my clients. Whether it’s crafting a visually stunning design or developing a powerful backend, I focus on delivering websites that look great, perform efficiently, and fit your specific needs.
