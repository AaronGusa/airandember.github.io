WHY AIR & EMBER

One craftsman, both halves of the craft.

I bring a blend of creativity and technical skill backed by a BS in Applied Technology and certifications in web development, computer programming, and system administration — with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript at the roots and MEAN/MERN stacks, Firebase, and Python in the canopy.

What sets Air & Ember apart is the seam you’ll never see: as both an artistic creative and a coding problem-solver, I eliminate the need for multiple teams. One pair of hands carries your project from sketch to server — which keeps the process streamlined and the costs manageable.
