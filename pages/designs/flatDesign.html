FLAT DESIGN

Flat web design is a minimalist approach emphasizing clean lines, solid colors, and 2D elements without textures or gradients. Its advantages include faster load times, responsive adaptability, and user-friendly interfaces. By avoiding excessive design clutter, it enhances readability, simplifies navigation, and creates a modern aesthetic that aligns with digital trends.
