WHY WE EXIST

01

You were promised the moon

A glossy deck. A confident sales team. A number that sounded almost reasonable. The enterprise agency made it look effortless — because closing you was the only part they had actually rehearsed.

02

Then the fire went out

Deadlines slipped. The scope you agreed to became a list of expensive “extras.” Your emails fell into a ticket queue, answered three time zones away by someone who never learned your business's name. The embers went grey.

03

One honest spark — transparency

We begin the opposite way. Real quotes, line by line. A working prototype before a dollar moves. You see exactly what's being built, what it costs, and why — no black box, no surprise invoice. One spark of honesty catches.

04

We feed it — education

We refuse to gatekeep the tech to keep you dependent. We teach you how your site and tools actually work, in plain language, so you own your business instead of renting it back from us. Knowledge is the kindling.

05

Faith, rekindled — fairness

Fair pricing. Weekly check-ins. A partner who is still here long after launch. One small business at a time, we turn “burned by developers” back into “this is how it should have felt all along.” The fire is warm again.

Transparency · Education · Fairness. The three logs we never let burn down.
